About Thick & True

Building confidence through science-backed hair care for New Zealand men.

Our Story

Thick & True was founded with a simple mission: to make effective hair loss treatments accessible and understandable for everyday New Zealand men. We believe that confidence starts with feeling good about how you look, and that everyone deserves access to treatments that work.

Unlike overseas brands that can be confusing or intimidating, we focus on plain language, local compliance, and products that are suitable for sale in New Zealand pharmacies.

Our Approach

Science-backed, not hype

We only sell products with clinical evidence. No miracle cures or unproven ingredients.

New Zealand focused

All products are over-the-counter items available in NZ pharmacies. We comply with Medsafe regulations.

Honest communication

We explain how treatments work, realistic timelines, and potential side effects in plain language.

No subscriptions required

You control your orders. Optional subscriptions available for convenience, but cancel anytime.

Our Team

We're a small team of pharmacists, trichologists, and developers working to make hair care simpler and more effective. Based in Auckland, we understand the unique needs of Kiwi men.
