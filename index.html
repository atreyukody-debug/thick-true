What to expect

Hair recovery takes time. Here's a realistic timeline based on clinical studies and customer feedback.

Month 1-2

Treatment begins

Minoxidil starts working immediately at the follicle level. Some initial shedding may occur as weaker hairs make way for stronger growth. This is normal and a sign the treatment is working.

Month 3

Shedding slows

Hair loss begins to slow. You may notice less hair in the shower or on your pillow. Early vellus (fine) hairs may appear in thinning areas.

Month 6

Visible thickening

Most men see noticeable improvement by month 6. Hairs appear thicker and denser. Hairline may show signs of filling in. This is when many men start getting comments.

Month 12

Peak results

Maximum results typically achieved by 12 months. Continued use maintains gains. Stopping treatment will gradually reverse progress over 3-6 months.

Important: Individual results vary. Consistency is key — missing applications significantly reduces effectiveness. Read full medicine information.

Frequently asked questions

How long does delivery take?

North Island: 1-2 business days. South Island: 2-4 business days. Rural deliveries may add 1-2 days. All orders ship from NZ.

Do I need a prescription?

No. All products we sell are over-the-counter (OTC) medicines legally available in New Zealand pharmacies. No doctor's visit or prescription required.

Is the packaging discreet?

Yes. All orders ship in plain, unbranded packaging with no mention of Thick & True or hair products on the exterior.

How long until I see results?

Most men notice reduced shedding by month 3 and visible thickening by month 6. Maximum results typically occur around 12 months. Consistency is essential — skipping applications significantly reduces effectiveness.

What if it doesn't work for me?

Minoxidil works for approximately 80-92% of men when used consistently. If you don't see results after 6 months of proper use, consult a doctor or dermatologist. We accept returns of unopened products within 30 days.

Can I use Afterpay?

Yes — Afterpay is available at checkout for orders over $35. Your order ships immediately while you pay in four fortnightly instalments.

Are there side effects?

The most common side effect is scalp irritation or dryness, usually mild and temporary. Read our medicine information for full details. Stop use and consult a doctor if you experience chest pain, dizziness, or rapid heartbeat.
