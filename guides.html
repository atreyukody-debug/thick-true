Starter Kit: Daily Routine

A simple 5‑minute routine using Nioxin + 5% Minoxidil Foam.

Morning routine (2–3 minutes)

  1. Shampoo with Nioxin. Massage into the scalp for 30–60 seconds.
  2. Condition lightly. Focus on the scalp, not just the hair.
  3. Towel dry. Your scalp should be dry before applying minoxidil.
  4. Apply minoxidil foam. Part your hair and apply a small amount to thinning areas.

Evening routine (1–2 minutes)

  1. Ensure your scalp is dry. Minoxidil works best on a dry scalp.
  2. Apply minoxidil foam again. Same areas as the morning.
  3. Let it absorb. Avoid lying down for ~10 minutes.

Consistency matters more than anything. Missing days slows progress.

How to Use Minoxidil (Foam or Solution)

Plain‑language instructions for safe, effective use.

Step‑by‑step

  1. Start with a dry scalp. Water dilutes the product.
  2. Part your hair. Expose the thinning area.
  3. Apply the foam/solution. Use only the recommended amount.
  4. Massage gently. Spread it across the scalp — don’t rub aggressively.
  5. Wash hands afterwards. Avoid touching your face or eyes.

Tips for best results

  • Use every day — results take months, not weeks.
  • Take photos every 3 months to track progress.
  • Expect some initial shedding — this is normal.
  • Don’t apply more than recommended; it won’t speed things up.

Always read the label and follow directions for use. Talk to your doctor or pharmacist if unsure.

Hairline Recovery Timeline

What most men experience over 12 months of consistent treatment.

Month‑by‑month expectations

  • Month 1–2: Scalp feels healthier. Some shedding may occur.
  • Month 3–4: Early signs of regrowth — small, thin hairs.
  • Month 5–6: Noticeable thickening in treated areas.
  • Month 7–9: Hairline looks fuller; density improves.
  • Month 10–12: Strongest results. Routine becomes automatic.

Results vary. Genetics, consistency, and lifestyle all play a role.
