Specialist Scalp Shampoo

Medicated shampoo for scalp health, helps control dandruff and irritation.

Key benefits

  • Anti-dandruff formula: Helps control flaking and itching.
  • Gentle cleansing: Suitable for daily use on sensitive scalps.
  • pH balanced: Maintains scalp's natural balance.

How to use

  1. Wet hair: Thoroughly wet your hair and scalp.
  2. Apply shampoo: Massage into scalp, leave for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Rinse well: Rinse completely with warm water.
  4. Use 2-3 times weekly: Or as directed for your scalp condition.

For external use only. If symptoms persist, consult a doctor or pharmacist.

Common questions

Is this for dandruff only?

This shampoo helps with dandruff and general scalp irritation, which can affect hair health.

Can I use it with other treatments?

Yes, use as part of your hair care routine. Apply treatments after shampooing.
