How to use (plain language)

  1. Morning: Wash hair with Nioxin shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry.
  2. Apply foam: Part your hair, apply a small amount of minoxidil foam to the thinning areas, and gently rub in.
  3. Evening: Apply minoxidil foam again to the same areas on a dry scalp.
  4. Stay consistent: Use every day. It can take several months to see visible change.

Always read the label and follow directions for use. If irritation occurs, stop use and talk to your doctor or pharmacist.

Common questions

How long until I see results?

Some men notice changes in 3–6 months, but it can take longer. Hair growth is slow and gradual.

Will this work for everyone?

These products are designed for hereditary hair loss in men. Results vary. If you’re unsure, talk to your doctor or pharmacist.
