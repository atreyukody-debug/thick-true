Medicine Information

Important information about our products

Minoxidil Foam

Active Ingredient: Minoxidil 5%

Indications: For the treatment of male pattern baldness (androgenetic alopecia) in men.

Directions: Apply 1g (half a capful) twice daily to the affected areas of the scalp. Massage gently.

Warnings: For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use. Consult a doctor if you have heart conditions.

Rosemary Oil

Active Ingredient: Rosemary oil extract

Indications: Traditional remedy for scalp health and hair growth support.

Directions: Apply a few drops to scalp and massage. Use daily or as needed.

Warnings: For external use only. Patch test before use. Discontinue if irritation occurs.

Specialist Shampoo

Active Ingredients: Ketoconazole 2%, caffeine, biotin

Indications: For scalp health and hair strengthening.

Directions: Wet hair, apply shampoo, massage into scalp, rinse thoroughly. Use 2-3 times per week.

Warnings: Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation persists, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional.

General Information

All products are available over-the-counter in New Zealand. Results may vary. Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

Always read the label and follow directions. If symptoms persist, consult your healthcare professional.
