Rosemary Scalp Oil

Natural oil blend to stimulate scalp circulation and support healthy hair growth.

Key benefits

  • Rosemary extract: Known to improve blood circulation to the scalp.
  • Essential oils: Lavender and peppermint for soothing and refreshing.
  • Light formula: Absorbs quickly without greasiness.

How to use

  1. Apply to scalp: Massage a few drops into your scalp, focusing on thinning areas.
  2. Leave on: Let it absorb for 30 minutes or overnight.
  3. Rinse: Wash hair as usual.
  4. Use 2-3 times weekly: For best results, combine with your hair loss routine.

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use.

Common questions

Is this safe for all hair types?

Yes, this natural oil blend is suitable for most hair types. Patch test first if you have sensitive skin.

Can I use it with minoxidil?

Yes, apply after minoxidil has dried to avoid interaction.
