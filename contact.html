Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

North Island: 1–2 business days. South Island: 2–4 business days. Rural deliveries may add 1–2 days.

Do you offer refunds?

Unopened products can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Opened products are not eligible for return due to hygiene reasons.

Is minoxidil safe?

5% minoxidil is an approved OTC treatment in New Zealand. Read our full medicine information for usage instructions and side effects.

Can I use Afterpay?

Yes — Afterpay is available at checkout for orders over $35. Your order ships immediately while you pay in four fortnightly instalments.
